TCF Vendor Purposes

This page shows the standard purposes defined in IAB Europe's Transparency & Consent Framework.

Standard Purposes

Vendors can select different legal bases for these purposes (Consent or Legitimate Interest).

P1 Store and/or access information on a device
Cookies, device identifiers, or other information can be stored or accessed on your device for the purposes presented to you.
Important: Purpose 1 (Store and/or access information on a device) requires explicit Consent under the ePrivacy Directive. Legitimate Interest is not a valid legal basis for this purpose regardless of what vendors may claim.
Legal Basis: Consent only Legitimate Interest Not Allowed (ePrivacy Directive)
P2 Use basic data to select ads
Ads can be shown to you based on the content you're viewing, the app you're using, your approximate location, or your device type.
Legal Basis: Consent or Legitimate Interest
P3 Create profiles for personalized ads
Profiles can be built about you and your interests to show you personalized ads that are relevant to you.
Legal Basis: Consent or Legitimate Interest
P4 Select personalized ads
Personalized ads can be shown to you based on profiles about your interests.
Legal Basis: Consent
P5 Create profiles for personalized content
Profiles can be built about you and your interests to show you personalized content that is relevant to you.
Legal Basis: Consent or Legitimate Interest
P6 Select personalized content
Personalized content can be shown to you based on profiles about your interests.
Legal Basis: Consent or Legitimate Interest
P7 Measure ad performance
The effectiveness of ads that you see or interact with can be measured.
Legal Basis: Consent or Legitimate Interest
P8 Measure content performance
The effectiveness of content that you see or interact with can be measured.
Legal Basis: Consent or Legitimate Interest
P9 Apply market research to generate audience insights
Market research can be used to learn more about the audiences who visit sites/apps and view ads.
Legal Basis: Consent or Legitimate Interest
P10 Develop and improve products
Your data can be used to improve existing systems and software, and to develop new products.
Legal Basis: Consent or Legitimate Interest

Special Purposes

These purposes allow vendors to process data based on Legitimate Interest without asking for consent.

SP1 Ensure security, prevent fraud, and debug
Your data can be used to monitor for and prevent fraudulent activity, and ensure systems and processes work properly and securely.
Legal Basis: Legitimate Interest
SP2 Technically deliver ads or content
Your device can receive and send information that allows you to see and interact with ads and content.
Legal Basis: Legitimate Interest

Features

These features are used in conjunction with the purposes above.

F1 Match and combine offline data sources
Data from offline data sources can be combined with your online activity in support of one or more purposes.
F2 Link different devices
Different devices can be determined as belonging to you or your household in support of one or more purposes.
F3 Receive and use automatically-sent device characteristics for identification
Your device might be distinguished from other devices based on information it automatically sends, such as IP address or browser type.

Special Features

These features always require explicit consent from the user.

SF1 Use precise geolocation data
Your precise geolocation data can be used in support of one or more purposes.
Legal Basis: Consent
SF2 Actively scan device characteristics for identification
Your device can be identified based on a scan of your device's unique combination of characteristics.
Legal Basis: Consent
